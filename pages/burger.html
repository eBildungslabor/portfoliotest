Eat-da-Burger

A fully-responsive, full-stack restaurant application.

Eat-da-Burger allows you to view, order, and consume your favorite burgers. Click the knife and fork to eat any burger in stock (left). Click the waiter to order more of any burger out of stock (right). Use the form to order a unique burger of your own.

Instructions

You can play with Eat-da-Burger on Heroku.

You will need to have the following Node.js packages installed prior to running this application locally:

  • body-parser
  • express
  • express-handlebars
  • mysql

To run Eat-da-Burger locally, navigate to its directory and enter "node server.js" in the command line.
